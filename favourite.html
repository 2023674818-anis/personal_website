Falling for Life in Yellow

Of the many colors that exist, the one that catches my eye the most is yellow. I like it because it's bright and shiny. I also learned about the symbolism of the color yellow. It is said that yellow represents joy, optimism, and intellectual energy. It symbolizes the warmth of friendship, the excitement of new ideas, and the confidence that comes from mental clarity. Indirectly, it makes me love things like sunsets and sunflowers. This color can give me the peace and new energy that I need when I am under stress. In addition, this color can be combined with any color and it becomes beautiful and stylish. For example, yellow and purple.
